Dune

Genre: Science Fiction

Description: Set on the desert planet Arrakis, Dune is the story of the boy Paul Atreides, heir to a noble family tasked with ruling an inhospitable world where the only thing of value is the "spice" melange, a drug capable of extending life and enhancing consciousness. Coveted across the known universe, melange is a prize worth killing for. When House Atreides is betrayed, the destruction of Paul's family will set the boy on a journey toward a destiny greater than he ever could have imagined.

Pride and Prejudice

Genre: Romance

Description: Elizabeth Bennet, the second of five daughters in a respectable but not wealthy family, must navigate the rigid expectations of English society. When the wealthy and proud Mr. Darcy arrives in her neighborhood, first impressions create misunderstanding and friction between them. As pride and prejudice on both sides are gradually softened, Elizabeth and Darcy come to see each other in a truer light.

The Hobbit

Genre: Fantasy

Description: Bilbo Baggins is a hobbit who enjoys a comfortable, unambitious life, rarely traveling further than the pantry of his hobbit-hole. But his contentment is disturbed when the wizard Gandalf and a company of thirteen dwarves arrive on his doorstep one day to whisk him away on an adventure. They have launched a plot to raid the treasure hoard guarded by the dragon Smaug, and Bilbo reluctantly joins their quest, sharpening his wits along a journey that takes him from his hobbit-hole to the Lonely Mountain.
