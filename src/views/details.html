Pride and Prejudice

Genre: Romance

Author: Jane Austen

Year: 1813

Rating: ★★★★

Description: Elizabeth Bennet, the second of five daughters in a respectable but not wealthy family, must navigate the rigid expectations of English society. When the wealthy and proud Mr. Darcy arrives in her neighborhood, first impressions create misunderstanding and friction between them. As pride and prejudice on both sides are gradually softened, Elizabeth and Darcy come to see each other in a truer light.
